Why spend 5 minutes doing a task when you can spend a couple hours automating it? Last semester, I filled in my timetable into my calendar manually.

This semester, I decided not to and built an extension to download my timetable into .ics format by fiddling with the website's HTML with a Chrome Extension.

This is not endorsed, encouraged or in any way affiliated with the Macquarie University. It's just something I made for my own convenience.
