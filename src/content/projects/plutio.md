## Case Study

[Plutio](https://plutio.com) was my second Upwork client and we initially started with a $100 debugging session, and after the CEO found me great to work with, we started a 5-month collaboration where I diagnosed any issues they had on their monorepo NodeJs deployment and fixed up their cloud environments across AWS, Google Cloud, Vultr and Digital Ocean, optimizing costs where I could.

It's currently facing some system instability problems due to a massive refactor to help with the AI integration, and hopefully will soon be stable. Our cooperation ended as a cost-saving measure after considerable investment into AI development.

No tech stack, architecture or coding problems can be disclosed due to the nature of the job in the interest of security.
